Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to ConfidentCam ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name (first name only, for personalization)
  • Age (for tailored content)
  • Location (city/region, for cultural context)
  • Communication goals and preferences
  • Language preference

Content Data:

  • Videos you record using the app (stored locally on your device)
  • Progress data and completion status

Technical Data:

  • Device information
  • App usage analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalized Scripts: We use your name, age, location, and goals to generate customized speaking practice scripts via AI.
  • Progress Tracking: To track your 35-day challenge progress and provide achievements.
  • App Improvement: To improve our services and user experience.
  • Communication: To send you daily reminders (if enabled) and important updates.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure authentication and data storage
  • OpenAI: For generating personalized speaking scripts (only your preferences are shared, not videos)

Important: Your recorded videos are stored locally on your device and are NOT uploaded to our servers or any third-party service unless you explicitly choose to share them.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Personal data is stored securely on Supabase with encryption
  • Videos remain on your device only
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • You can delete your data at any time through the app settings

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Data Portability: Export your data

To exercise these rights, use the "Delete Account" option in Settings or contact us.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as you maintain an active account. When you delete your account:

  • All personal data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Local videos on your device are deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

8. Children's Privacy

ConfidentCam is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, for significant changes, through in-app notifications.
